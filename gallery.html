About Tivat

Tivat is situated in the central part of Boka Kotorska. From the north it is protected by the Mountain Lovcen (1749 m) and Lovcen`s spur Vrmac (710 m). Tivat bay through gorge Verige is connected with Risan and Kotor bay. Tivat, whose total surface is 49 km2 is considered to be the most wide spread lodgment in Boka Kotorska.
